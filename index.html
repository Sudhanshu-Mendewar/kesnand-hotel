Our Story

Hotel Kesnand Village is a popular local hotel known for its delicious veg and non-veg thali meals. Established with a passion for authentic Indian cuisine, we have been serving our community for years with traditional recipes passed down through generations.

We specialize in flavorful chicken and mutton dishes prepared with authentic spices and fresh ingredients. Our chefs carefully select and blend spices to create dishes that burst with flavor and aroma.
