About Us

Our story, values, and commitment to quality

Our Story

Hotel Kesnand Village is a popular local hotel known for its delicious veg and non-veg thali meals. Established with a passion for authentic Indian cuisine, we have been serving our community for years with traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Founded in 1995 by Mr. Rajesh Pawar, our hotel started as a small eatery with just 5 tables. Over the years, we've grown into a beloved local institution, but we've never lost sight of our roots. Our commitment to quality, taste, and hospitality remains as strong as ever.

Our Philosophy

We believe that good food brings people together, and we strive to create a welcoming environment where families and friends can enjoy delicious meals and create lasting memories. Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest ingredients, follow traditional recipes, and serve every customer with respect and care.

Our Specialties

We specialize in flavorful chicken and mutton dishes prepared with authentic spices and fresh ingredients. Our chefs carefully select and blend spices to create dishes that burst with flavor and aroma, ensuring every meal is a memorable experience.

Quality & Service

The hotel provides a comfortable dining atmosphere, quality food, and prompt service at reasonable prices. We maintain strict quality control measures and source our ingredients from trusted local suppliers to ensure consistency and freshness in every dish we serve.

Our Team

Behind every great meal is a dedicated team. Our chefs have decades of combined experience in Indian cuisine, while our service staff is trained to provide warm, attentive service. We're proud of our team and consider them family.

Community Involvement

As a local business, we believe in giving back to our community. We regularly participate in local festivals, support community events, and provide catering services for special occasions at discounted rates for our regular customers.

At Hotel Kesnand Village, our commitment to quality, taste, and hospitality is what sets us apart. We invite you to experience the authentic flavors of India with us.

Our Values
