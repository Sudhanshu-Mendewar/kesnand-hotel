About Our Chicken Dishes

What makes our chicken specialties exceptional

Fresh Chicken

We use fresh, locally sourced chicken that is cleaned and prepared daily. Our chicken is always tender and cooked to perfection.

Our chefs prepare special spice blends in-house using traditional recipes. Each dish has its unique spice combination.
