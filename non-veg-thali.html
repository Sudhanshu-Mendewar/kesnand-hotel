About Our Non-Veg Thali

What makes our non-vegetarian thali special

Fresh Meat Selection

We source fresh chicken, mutton, and seafood daily from trusted suppliers. Our meat is carefully cleaned and prepared following strict hygiene standards.

Authentic Spice Blends

Our secret spice blends are prepared in-house using traditional recipes. We balance flavors to create dishes that are rich, aromatic, and perfectly spiced.

Slow Cooking Methods

Many of our dishes are slow-cooked to perfection, allowing flavors to develop fully and meat to become tender and juicy.

Variety & Balance

Each thali offers a balanced combination of protein-rich meat dishes, carbohydrates, and accompaniments to provide a complete and satisfying meal.

Hotel Kesnand Village

A popular local hotel known for delicious veg and non-veg thali meals. We specialize in flavorful chicken and mutton dishes prepared with authentic spices and fresh ingredients.
